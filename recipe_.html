Salt Grilled Chicken

by Mr.Carnivore

Ingredients

  • 1 ts Sea salt
  • 1 ts Pepper
  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 0.5 ts Parsley
  • 2 ts Olive Oil
  • 1 ts Garlic Powder
  • 1 ts Smoked paprika

Time Consuming

  • Preparing Time: About 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: About 30 minutes

Cost

  • $10~20 (depends on chicken you choose)

Instructions


  1. Heat oven to medium
  2. In a bowl, season chicken breast with salt and pepper and wait 30 min
  3. Add parsely, garlic powder, and smoked paprika in a bowl
  4. Put chicken in grill and grill each side for 3 minutes.
  5. Put olive oil in the pan, and sear each side for 2 minutes each
  6. Make sure the meat is fully cooked
